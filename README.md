# SBI AURA

### **Agentic AI-Powered Digital Adoption Coach**

**Theme:** Agentic AI & Emerging Technologies

**Problem Statement:** Digital Adoption

**Tagline:** *Transforming First-Time Banking Customers into Confident Digital Banking Users.*

---

## Executive Summary

Despite significant investments in digital banking, many first-time customers stop engaging after opening an account. They struggle to navigate banking applications, understand financial products, and confidently use digital services. Existing onboarding relies on static campaigns, generic notifications, and reactive chatbots that fail to adapt to individual customer journeys.

**SBI AURA** is an **Agentic AI-powered Digital Adoption Coach** that focuses on the **first 30 days** of a customer's banking journey—the period with the highest impact on long-term digital engagement. Instead of promoting products, AURA autonomously guides customers toward meaningful banking milestones through personalized, adaptive, and context-aware journeys.

The solution combines **Agentic AI, behavioral analytics, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and SBI-approved knowledge** to improve digital adoption while remaining secure, explainable, and compliant.

---

## The Problem

### Current Reality

India has witnessed exponential digital banking growth, with **UPI contributing over 80% of retail digital payment volume**, yet RBI continues to identify **digital adoption, literacy, awareness, and trust** as major challenges.

SBI has successfully digitized its services through YONO, UPI, Internet Banking, and Mobile Banking. However, availability does not guarantee adoption.

The biggest drop-off occurs immediately after onboarding.

**Typical customer journey:**

```
Account Opened
        ↓
Downloads YONO
        ↓
Explores App
        ↓
Gets Overwhelmed
        ↓
Stops Using Digital Services
```

Most first-time customers:
* Never activate UPI
* Never register Internet Banking
* Never explore beyond basic payments
* Never adopt additional digital products

Industry research also shows that the **first 30–90 days determine long-term customer engagement**, making early digital adoption the highest-leverage intervention.

### Evidence
* UPI accounts for over **80% of India's retail digital payments** (PIB, Ministry of Finance).
* RBI identifies **digital literacy, awareness, and adoption** as ongoing challenges.
* SBI reports **92% overall digital adoption**, but improving active engagement remains a strategic objective.
* Research indicates users completing **5 digital transactions within the first week are twice as likely to remain active** (Women's World Banking).

```
Rule Engine

        ↓
Send SMS
```
Every customer receives the same journey. No memory. No personalization. No adaptation.

## Why Agentic AI?

Digital adoption is a **goal**, not a single interaction.

Unlike chatbots that answer questions or CRM engines that execute predefined rules, Agentic AI continuously works toward achieving customer adoption milestones.

| CRM                 | Chatbot             | SBI AURA                   |
| ------------------- | ------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Static workflows    | Answers questions   | Pursues adoption goals     |
| No long-term memory | Session-based       | Persistent customer memory |
| Same journey        | Reactive            | Personalized journey       |
| Rule execution      | Response generation | Plans, adapts, and learns  |

## Proposed Solution: SBI AURA

An autonomous Digital Adoption Coach embedded within YONO.

Its mission is simple:
> **Increase each customer's Digital Adoption Score during the first 30 days.**

Rather than teaching finance through courses, AURA integrates learning directly into real banking tasks.

**Example:**
```
Customer wants to activate UPI
        ↓
AI explains MPIN
        ↓
Guides setup
        ↓
Performs ₹1 demo transaction
        ↓
Explains benefits
        ↓
Moves to next milestone
```

## Cost Optimization

To remain scalable for SBI's customer base, AURA uses a **tiered AI architecture**.

```
Simple FAQ
        ↓
Knowledge Search
        ↓
Small Language Model

Complex Journey
        ↓
Planning Agent
        ↓
Large Language Model
```
This minimizes inference costs while reserving advanced reasoning for high-value interactions.

## Running the Prototype Locally

This repository contains a high-fidelity front-end React prototype of the SBI AURA experience. It focuses on demonstrating the 5–10 minute journey of a new user onboarding via the Agentic AI.

**To run the application:**

1. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```

2. Start the development server:
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```

3. Open the provided `localhost` URL in your browser. *(Note: The UI is optimized for a mobile experience. Use your browser's Developer Tools Mobile Device Toggle for the best viewing experience.)*
